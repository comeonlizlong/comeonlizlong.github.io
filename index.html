About Me

I am currently a Master’s student in Information and Communication Engineering at Hunan Institute of Science and Technology, and I will be starting my Ph.D. program in September 2026.

My research aims to move beyond passive adaptation and focus on the active reshaping of physical channels in 6G wireless networks. I am particularly passionate about exploring the degrees of freedom provided by novel antenna architectures, such as Movable Antennas (MA) and Rotatable Antennas (RA), and optimizing these systems using Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL).

Research Interests

  • 6G Wireless Communications:Movable Antennas (MA), Rotatable Antennas (RA)
  • Smart Radio Environments:Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS)
  • Resource Allocation & Trajectory Optimization:Deep Reinforcement Learning (DQN, DDPG, PPO)
  • Next-Generation Paradigms:Semantic Communications, Deep JSCC
  • Physical Layer Security:Covert Communications
